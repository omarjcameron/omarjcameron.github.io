Habits

Habits is your catered daily journaling application. When you query the habits of many highly successful people, one common denominator stands out - journaling and taking the time to reflect daily. Habits allows you monitor your habits day by day, by creating questions you'd like to answer based on this habits. Users can provide boolean, rating and text responses to simple SMS prompts which in turn translates into meaningful data depicting a users performance on their designated 'habit'. Habits is a decoupled application featuring a Ruby/Rails and Javascript backend with web applicaiton views, as well as a React Native mobile view. Habits incorporates various APIs such as Twilio, GoogleMaps, and WeatherUnderground. Other features include catered statistics and geotracking.
