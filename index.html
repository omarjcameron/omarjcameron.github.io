What is traveling hacking?

Travel hacking is the art of hustling and scheming to procure free and or discounted travel through the use of frequent flier points, credit card bonuses, rewards programs and savvy search mechanisms.

Source: Chris Jarvis

Can I be a travel hacker?

Absolutely! Most of travel hacking comes to simply being in the know! The hardest thing about travel hacking isn't putting your plans and schemes to action, it's really just finding out about them in the first place!
